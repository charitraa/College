Course

1. Management

i. overview

The management field is as challenging as any other. Students who wish to make management their career must devote themselves to their studies from the very beginning. The AMC serves as the right backdrop for their endeavour. The Course teaches students the basic concepts of management as it prepares them for getting admission to renowned colleges. It intensively focuses on the fundamentals of Accountancy, Mathematics, Economics, Business Studies, and English.

ii. Future Opportunities

Here are the top seven career options in Management that you should look out for :

  • Marketing Manager
  • Sales Manager
  • Financial Manager
  • Computer and Information Systems Manager
  • Public Relations Manager
  • Human Resources Manager
  • General Manager

ELIGIBILITY

Students seeking to enroll in Plus Two Management must score the following in SEE :

  • GPA: 1.6
  • At least D+ in English, Nepali, Mathematics, Science and Social Studies.
